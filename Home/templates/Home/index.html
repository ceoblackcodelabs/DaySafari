Welcome To Day Safaris Adventures

Explore East Africa with Day Safaris – Become a Story Teller

Day Safaris Adventures is Kenya's leading and fastest-growing tour company, renowned for delivering exceptional safari experiences across East Africa. As a premier destination management company, we specialize in creating unforgettable wildlife adventures, cultural immersions, and luxury getaways throughout Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, and Rwanda.

With our headquarters in Nairobi, Kenya, and strategic partner offices in South Africa and Tanzania, we offer tailor-made safari packages that include private game drives, hot air balloon safaris, mountain climbing expeditions, and beach holidays at the Kenyan coast. Our commitment to sustainable tourism and exceptional service ensures every journey with us becomes a lifetime memory.

African Wildlife Safaris

Experience the magic of the African wilderness with our expert guides. Track the Big Five in Masai Mara, witness the Great Wildebeest Migration, and capture breathtaking moments of lions, elephants, rhinos, leopards, and buffalos in their natural habitat.

Travel Partnership Programs
