## DESTINATIONS

### NATIONAL PARKS & RESERVES

**Masai Mara National Reserve, Kenya**
- 📍 Location: Southwestern Kenya
- 📅 Best Time: July-October (Migration), Year-round (Wildlife)
- 🎯 Key Attractions: Great Wildebeest Migration, Big Five, Hot Air Balloon Safaris
- ✨ Unique Features: Over 1.5 million wildebeest during migration, Mara River crossings

**Serengeti National Park, Tanzania**
- 📍 Location: Northern Tanzania
- 📅 Best Time: December-July (Migration), Year-round
- 🎯 Key Attractions: Endless plains, River crossings, Kopjes, Big Cat sightings
- ✨ Unique Features: UNESCO World Heritage Site, Olduvai Gorge nearby

**Amboseli National Park, Kenya**
- 📍 Location: Loitoktok District, Kenya
- 📅 Best Time: June-October, January-February
- 🎯 Key Attractions: Mount Kilimanjaro views, Large elephant herds, Swamps
- ✨ Unique Features: Best elephant viewing in Africa, 1000+ elephants

**Tsavo National Park, Kenya**
- 📍 Location: Coast Province, Kenya
- 📅 Best Time: June-October, January-February
- 🎯 Key Attractions: Red elephants, Lava flows, Mzima Springs
- ✨ Unique Features: Kenya's largest national park, Divided into Tsavo East & West

**Bwindi Impenetrable National Park, Uganda**
- 📍 Location: Southwestern Uganda
- 📅 Best Time: June-August, December-February
- 🎯 Key Attractions: Mountain gorilla trekking, Forest hiking, Bird watching
- ✨ Unique Features: Half of world's mountain gorillas, UNESCO Site

**Queen Elizabeth National Park, Uganda**
- 📍 Location: Western Uganda
- 📅 Best Time: June-September, December-February
- 🎯 Key Attractions: Tree-climbing lions, Kazinga Channel, Crater lakes
- ✨ Unique Features: 95 mammal species, 600+ bird species

### BEACH DESTINATIONS

**Zanzibar Beach, Tanzania**
- 📍 Location: Zanzibar Archipelago
- 📅 Best Time: June-October, December-March
- 🎯 Activities: Snorkeling, Diving, Spice tours, Stone Town exploration
- ✨ Unique Features: UNESCO World Heritage Stone Town, Coral reefs

**Diani Beach, Kenya**
- 📍 Location: South Coast, Kenya
- 📅 Best Time: Year-round
- 🎯 Activities: Water sports, Diving, Waterskiing, Kite surfing
- ✨ Unique Features: 25km of white sand beach, Coral reefs

**Watamu Beach, Kenya**
- 📍 Location: North Coast, Kenya
- 📅 Best Time: Year-round
- 🎯 Activities: Snorkeling, Deep sea fishing, Dolphin watching
- ✨ Unique Features: Marine National Park, Turtle conservation

### MOUNTAIN TREKS

**Mount Kilimanjaro, Tanzania**
- ⛰️ Height: 5,895m (Africa's Highest Peak)
- 🚶 Routes: Machame, Marangu, Lemosho, Rongai
- ⏱️ Duration: 5-9 days
- 📅 Best Time: January-March, June-October
- 💪 Difficulty: Challenging

**Mount Kenya, Kenya**
- ⛰️ Height: 5,199m (Africa's 2nd Highest)
- 🚶 Routes: Sirimon, Chogoria, Naro Moru
- ⏱️ Duration: 3-5 days
- 📅 Best Time: January-February, August-September
- 💪 Difficulty: Moderate to Challenging

**Mount Rwenzori, Uganda**
- ⛰️ Height: 5,109m (Mountains of the Moon)
- 🚶 Routes: Central Circuit
- ⏱️ Duration: 7-9 days
- 📅 Best Time: June-August, December-February
- 💪 Difficulty: Very Challenging
